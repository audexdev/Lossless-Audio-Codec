# Code of Conduct

LAC is a small maintainer-run open source project. Contributors, maintainers, and users are expected to keep project discussion focused, respectful, and technical.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful when discussing bugs, design tradeoffs, and review feedback.
- Assume good intent, but be precise about technical risks and evidence.
- Keep issues and pull requests focused on reproducible project work.
- Avoid harassment, personal attacks, discriminatory language, or hostile behavior.
- Do not post exploit details, private crash inputs, or sensitive information in public threads.

## Maintainer Actions

Maintainers may edit, hide, lock, or remove comments that disrupt project work or expose sensitive security details. Repeated or severe violations may result in blocked participation.

Security-sensitive reports should follow `SECURITY.md`.
